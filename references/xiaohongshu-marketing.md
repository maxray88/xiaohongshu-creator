## Account Positioning

### Define Your Niche

Before posting, clearly define:
- **Target audience**: Who are you creating for? (age, interests, pain points)
- **Content pillars**: 3-5 topics you'll consistently cover
- **Unique angle**: What makes your perspective different?
- **Visual style**: Consistent aesthetic (colors, filters, layout)

### Profile Optimization

- **Avatar**: Clear, friendly face photo or branded logo
- **Nickname**: Memorable + searchable (include keywords if possible)
- **Bio**: Who you are + what value you provide + call to action
- **Account type**: Switch to creator account for analytics access

### Content Niche Examples

| Niche | Content Pillars | Target Audience |

| 旅行 | 攻略、打卡、摄影、美食 | 20-40 旅行爱好者 |

## Content Strategy

### The 3-3-3 Rule

For every 3 posts:
- **1 trending topic**: Ride a trending hashtag for exposure
- **1 evergreen topic**: Timeless content that gets search traffic
- **1 personal story**: Build connection and authenticity

### Content Calendar

**Optimal posting times** (China Standard Time):
- **Morning**: 7:00-9:00 (commute browsing)
- **Lunch**: 12:00-14:00 (break time)
- **Evening**: 20:00-22:00 (peak browsing)
- **Weekend**: 10:00-12:00, 15:00-17:00, 21:00-23:00

**Posting frequency**:
- New accounts: 3-4 posts/week
- Growing accounts: 4-5 posts/week
- Established accounts: 5-7 posts/week

### Content Types by Performance

1. **List posts** (「3个技巧」「5个方法」) — High save rate
2. **Tutorial/How-to** — High share rate
3. **Before/After** — High engagement
4. **Personal story** — High comment rate
5. **Review/Comparison** — High trust
6. **Trending topic** — High exposure

### Image Strategy

- **Cover image**: Bright, clear, text overlay with hook
- **Image count**: 3-9 images per post (more = more engagement)
- **Image quality**: Minimum 1080x1080, well-lit
- **Text on images**: Key points as bullet points
- **Consistency**: Use similar filters/colors across posts

## Hashtag Strategy

### Hashtag Types

1. **Broad tags** (100M+ views): #小红书 #好物分享 #生活分享
   - Use 1-2 per post
   - High competition, low targeting

2. **Niche tags** (1M-100M views): #护肤心得 #穿搭日记 #美食探店
   - Use 2-3 per post
   - Medium competition, good targeting

3. **Specific tags** (100K-1M views): #油皮护肤 #小个子穿搭 #一人食
   - Use 2-3 per post
   - Low competition, high conversion

4. **Trending tags** (time-sensitive): #高颜值巧克力 #早餐吃什么
   - Use 1-2 per post
   - Check daily on 笔记灵感

5. **Branded tags** (your own): #小明的美食日记
   - Use 1 per post
   - Build your own community

### Hashtag Research Process

1. Check 笔记灵感 daily for trending topics
2. Use `xhs_hashtags.py --analyze` to evaluate competition
3. Mix high-competition (exposure) + low-competition (targeting) tags
4. Track which hashtags drive the most views
5. Update your hashtag sets weekly

### Hashtag Placement

- Include hashtags at the end of your post body
- Don't put hashtags in the title (looks spammy)
- Maximum 10-15 hashtags per post
- Place most important hashtags first

## Engagement Strategy

### The 1:3 Rule

For every post you publish, engage with 3 other posts:
- Leave thoughtful comments (not just emojis)
- Like posts in your niche
- Follow accounts you genuinely admire

### Comment Strategy

**Good comments** (build connections):
- Ask a genuine question
- Share your own experience
- Add value to the discussion
- Be specific about what you liked

**Bad comments** (hurt your account):
- Generic: "好看！""赞！"
- Self-promotional: "来看看我的帖子"
- Spam: Copy-paste same comment everywhere

### Reply Strategy

- Reply to EVERY comment on your posts
- Reply within 1-2 hours (boosts engagement score)
- Ask follow-up questions to keep the conversation going
- Pin your best comments

### Community Building

1. **Find your tribe**: 10-20 accounts in your niche
2. **Engage daily**: Like and comment on their posts
3. **Collaborate**: Shoutouts, joint content, challenges
4. **Support newcomers**: Help new creators in your niche

## Analytics & Optimization

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | What It Tells You | Target |
|--------|-------------------|--------|
| 曝光数 (Exposure) | How many people saw your post | >500 for new accounts |
| 点击率 (CTR) | Cover image effectiveness | >5% |
| 点赞数 (Likes) | Content resonance | >2% of exposure |
| 评论数 (Comments) | Engagement depth | >0.5% of exposure |
| 收藏数 (Saves) | Content value | >1% of exposure |
| 分享数 (Shares) | Virality potential | >0.2% of exposure |
| 涨粉 (New followers) | Account growth | >10 per post |

### Weekly Analytics Review

Every Sunday, run:
```bash
$PYTHON xhs_analytics.py
```

And answer:
1. Which post performed best? Why?
2. Which hashtags drove the most exposure?
3. What time did my best post go live?
4. What content type got the most saves?
5. What should I do more of next week?

### A/B Testing

Test one variable at a time:
- **Titles**: Emotional hook vs. Number list vs. Question
- **Covers**: Face vs. Product vs. Text overlay
- **Posting times**: Morning vs. Afternoon vs. Evening
- **Hashtag sets**: Broad-heavy vs. Niche-heavy
- **Content types**: Tutorial vs. Story vs. List

Track results for 2-4 weeks before drawing conclusions.

## Growth Milestones

### 0-100 Followers (Foundation)
- Focus: Content quality + consistency
- Goal: Find your voice and niche
- Strategy: Post 3x/week, engage daily, optimize profile

### 100-1,000 Followers (Traction)
- Focus: Hashtag optimization + trending topics
- Goal: Get first viral post (1000+ likes)
- Strategy: Post 4x/week, research hashtags, join trends

### 1,000-10,000 Followers (Growth)
- Focus: Content series + community building
- Goal: Build loyal audience
- Strategy: Post 5x/week, create series, collaborate

### 10,000-100,000 Followers (Scale)
- Focus: Brand partnerships + monetization
- Goal: Become a recognized creator
- Strategy: Diversify content, start collaborations

### 100,000+ Followers (Authority)
- Focus: Brand building + business
- Goal: Full-time creator income
- Strategy: Team building, brand deals, own products

## Common Mistakes

### Content Mistakes
1. **Inconsistent posting**: Posting 5 times in one week, then nothing for a month
2. **Ignoring analytics**: Not tracking what works
3. **Copying others**: Lack of originality
4. **Too promotional**: Every post is an ad
5. **Low quality images**: Blurry, dark, or cluttered photos

### Engagement Mistakes
1. **Not replying to comments**: Kills engagement score
2. **Generic comments**: "好看！" on everything
3. **Follow/unfollow**: Desperate growth tactic that hurts credibility
4. **Ignoring DMs**: Missing connection opportunities

### Strategy Mistakes
1. **No niche**: Posting about everything
2. **Chasing trends blindly**: Joining trends unrelated to your niche
3. **Ignoring hashtags**: Using the same hashtags every time
4. **Giving up too soon**: Expecting viral results in the first month
5. **Not optimizing profile**: Incomplete bio, no clear value proposition

### Algorithm Mistakes
1. **Posting at wrong times**: When your audience isn't online
2. **Ignoring first 30 minutes**: Engagement in the first 30 min is critical
3. **Not using all features**: Ignoring polls, stickers, music
4. **Deleting and reposting**: Resets engagement history
5. **Buying fake engagement**: Destroys account credibility
